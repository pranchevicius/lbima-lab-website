LBIMA Lab

Laboratory of Bacterial Interactions and Microbial Applications

About Us

LBIMA Lab investigates bacterial interactions, microbial responses under stress, and applications in health and biotechnology. Our research focuses on bacterial ecology, resistance mechanisms, and innovative microbial solutions in industrial and clinical contexts.
