Our Research Focus

The LBIMA Lab focuses on cutting-edge research in molecular microbiology, immunology, bacterial interactions, antimicrobial resistance, and applied biotechnology. We study how bacteria interact with each other and with their hosts under different environmental and stress conditions, aiming to develop novel therapeutic strategies, vaccines, and biotechnological applications. Our work combines proteomics, immunoinformatics, and molecular analysis to understand microbial behavior and translate findings into practical solutions for human and animal health.

  • Antimicrobial resistance: molecular profiling of resistant pathogens and therapeutic approaches.
  • Microbial interactions and immunomodulation: studying competition, stress-induced physiological changes, and effects on host immune responses.
  • Biotechnology and vaccine design: using proteomics and immunoinformatics for next-generation therapeutics.
