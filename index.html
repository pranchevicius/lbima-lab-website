About Us

The LBIMA Lab studies predation-driven bacterial interactions, microbial applications, and stress-induced proteomic remodeling. We focus on understanding bacterial behavior under environmental and biological stresses, developing strategies for microbial applications in health and biotechnology, and fostering new collaborations to expand scientific knowledge.
