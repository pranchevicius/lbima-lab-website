About Us

The LBIMA Lab conducts innovative research in molecular microbiology, bacterial pathogenesis, immunology, and applied biotechnology. We investigate the immunomodulatory effects of clinically relevant Gram-negative bacteria, and develop recombinant vaccine candidates which are tested in vivo for immunogenicity and protective potential. Our research also includes studying bacterial profiles and resistance patterns in clinical infections to inform novel therapeutic strategies.
