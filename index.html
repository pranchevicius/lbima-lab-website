Laboratório de Biotecnologia, Imunologia e Microbiologia Aplicada (LBIMA)

About the Lab

The Laboratory of Biotechnology, Immunology and Applied Microbiology (LBIMA) at the Universidade Federal de São Carlos develops research focused on understanding the molecular, genetic and immunological mechanisms involved in the interaction between opportunistic and pathogenic bacteria and their hosts.

Research Areas

Phenotypic, Genetic and Genomic Characterization

Study of opportunistic and pathogenic bacteria including virulence mechanisms and antimicrobial resistance.

Vaccine Candidates and Drug Targets

Identification of bacterial molecules that may serve as vaccine candidates or therapeutic targets.

Host–Pathogen Interactions and Immune Response

Investigation of innate and adaptive immune responses to bacterial infections.
