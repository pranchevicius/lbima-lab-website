About the Lab

The LBIMA Lab studies bacterial interactions, antimicrobial resistance, and microbial ecology. Our research focuses on understanding how bacteria interact with each other and with hosts, with applications in microbiology, biotechnology, and health.

Research Areas

  • Bacterial predation
  • Host–microbe interactions
  • Antimicrobial resistance
  • Microbial ecology
